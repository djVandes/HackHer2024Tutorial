About us

Serve Kingston Youth is a Kingston-based humanitarian resource connecting both youth in need and youth hoping to volunteer
to local food banks and shelters. Advertised initiatives aim to provide essential support to those in need and provide awareness
to the Kingston community regarding the homeless youth population.

Explore Shelters to find general information for local organizations providing support and shelter,
as well as Food Banks to find meal-times and volunteer opportunities nearby.
