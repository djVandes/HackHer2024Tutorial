About us

This tool efficiently connects individuals with local food banks and shelters, streamlining access to crucial resources. Additionally, it provides a platform for those interested in community service to find volunteer opportunities. By utilizing this straightforward website, users can navigate essential information, contribute their time to support the homeless population, and play a role in fostering a more supportive community in Kingston.
