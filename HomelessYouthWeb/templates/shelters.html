Finding food and shelter locally

Home Base

This organization provides housing and support services to people experiencing homelessness or at risk of homelessness in Kingston. They often need volunteers to help with various tasks such as meal preparation, administrative work, fundraising events, and outreach programs.

Phone: 613-542-6672

Address: 540 Montreal St, Kingston, ON K7K 3J2

Interval House

While primarily focused on providing support and shelter to women and children fleeing domestic violence, Kingston Interval House may also offer volunteering opportunities that indirectly support individuals facing homelessness. Volunteers can assist with childcare, administrative tasks, fundraising events, and providing emotional support to residents.

Phone: (613) 546-1777 or 1 (800) 267-9445

Address: Kingston, ON K7L 5P5

Street Health Centre

Street Health Centre offers healthcare and support services to individuals experiencing homelessness or at risk of homelessness. They provide medical care, counseling, harm reduction services, and referrals to housing resources.

Phone: 613-549-1440

Address:115 Barrack Street, Kingston, ON K7K 0B4

Elizabeth Fry Society of Kingston

The Elizabeth Fry Society of Kingston offers housing support services and transitional housing for women involved in the criminal justice system or at risk of involvement. They provide housing assistance, counseling, advocacy, and life skills training.

Phone: 613-544-1744

Address: 127 Bagot Street, Kingston, ON K7L 3E9
